QSAM: Quantum Sensing and Matter

At the intersection of solid state physics, chemistry, and quantum information and metrology, we create systems that host many-mody quantum phases and explore their properties using quantum sensor microscopes.

Our goal is to discover foundational properties of quantum matter, and to develop practical tools that harness them for applications relevant to quantum information, photonics, magnetics, and electronics.
