Outreach and Contact

Professor Maksimovic is committed to making research accessible to everyone, and understands that many pressures and challenges are present in academic spaces. We strive to have an open and supportive environment throughout our communities, from the lab to BU and beyond.

Undergraduate research opportunities, community programs, and mental health support are some of the causes essential to creating an inclusive community.We are open to and encourage all conversation - it is important to our group that everyone feels welcome!

If you are interested in joining or learning more, please contact Professor Maksimovic at nmak@bu.edu.
