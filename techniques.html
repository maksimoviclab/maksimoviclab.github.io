Materials Synthesis

Solution-based and vapor-based crystal growth techniques are used to synthesize bulk single crystals, as well as atomically-thin sheets and nanowires.

Cryogenic Measurement Techniques



We use a variety of low-temperature physical properties measurements techniques, including electrical transport in bulk samples, quantum transport in mesoscopic devices, calorimetry, and magnetometry, to investigate many-body phenomena in materials we create.




Optics and Microwave



Quantum sensing using color centers in semiconductors requires precise timing between optical and microwave pulses. We use a variety of optical and microwave engineering techniques in order to develop sensing modalities based on quantum defects over a wide range of environmental conditions.
